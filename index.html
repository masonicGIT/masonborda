Background
2008
Boeing & Space Systems/Loral
Electrical engineer. Worked on aerospace systems after graduating from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo (BS) and Santa Clara University (MS, Electrical Engineering).
2013
BitGo — First Ethereum Custody
Entered crypto. Built the first commercially viable Ethereum custody solution. Co-created Ether.li — the first Ethereum multi-signature web wallet — with Ben Chan and Barath Chandrashekar. Also architected custody infrastructure for the Royal Mint of England's blockchain project.
2014
Co-founded Payroll Integrations
Co-founded Payroll Integrations, which today serves clients representing 14% of the U.S. 401(k) market.
2017
Founded Tokensoft
Founded Tokensoft the week the SEC issued its first blockchain securities guidance. First customers launched September 9, 2017 — the same day as incorporation. Invented ERC-1404, the token standard for SEC-compliant blockchain securities.
2019
SEC Transfer Agent Registration
Tokensoft secured SEC registration as a transfer agent through subsidiary DTAC LLC — building toward the vision of an "automated investment bank" on the blockchain. Partnerships with Coinbase Custody, tZERO, Signature Bank, Anchorage, SEBA Bank.
2020
Historic Firsts — ArCoin & INX IPO
Led the first Ethereum-native investment fund registered under the '40 Act with the SEC (ArCoin). Led the first SEC-registered security token IPO in history (INX, ~$85M raised on Ethereum). Featured in CoinDesk and Forbes.
2021
Wrapped XRP & Cross-Chain Expansion
Launched Wrapped XRP (wXRP) on Ethereum DeFi via Tokensoft's Wrapped product. Expanded cross-chain bridging to Kadena and other networks.
